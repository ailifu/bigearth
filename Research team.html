13 Climate Action

JIA Gensuo

JIA Gensuo is a professor at the Institute of Atmospheric Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) and the Director of CAS Global Change Research Center for East Asia (START TEA). Prof. Jia currently serves as member of the International Science Council (ISC) regional committee for Asia-Pacific, coordinating lead author (CLA) of IPCC Special Report on Climate Change and Land (SRCCL), CLA of UNEP Global Environmental Outlook (GEO-6), deputy co-chair of Group on Earth Observation (GEO) climate change working group. Prof. Jia has broad research interests in terrestrial ecology and atmospheric sciences, including multi-scale remote sensing of land surface processes, ecosystem-climate interactions, climate extremes and natural disasters, and climate change impacts and adaptation.
